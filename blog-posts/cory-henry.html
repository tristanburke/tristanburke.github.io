I will be the first to admit I have a unhealthy relationship with Youtube. As one of the four sites I have bookmarked (Github, FB, Reddit, IsitChristmas), I visit Youtube daily. Although I would argue I have learned a lot on Youtube, it has undoubtedly been a black hole of procrastination, perpetrated by the astonishingly accurate "Recommended" and "Related Videos" side bar. I often find hours can fly by as video after video magically presents itself to me on any given topic, all perfectly titled to perk my interest and prolong a quick break into an hour long video watching montage.

It was during one of these endless video sessions that I came across a video title "Cory Henry visits Casio at NAMM".
