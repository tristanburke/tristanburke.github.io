Projects


The Classroom, VR Application: My most recent project in VR, The Classroom is an interactive educational experience. I built the application in Unity, and modeled many of the assets myself in Blender. The first . I started by implementing a class in the solar system. The user learns about the planets of our solar system, with the ability to pick up and hold each planet, as well .

 
 

Huddle, VR Application: A project I devised with my brother during the middle of the pandemic, Huddle is an application allowing users to meet in a virtual business room. Once again, I built the project in Unity and modeled all of the assets in Blender. I utilized Unity's Photon Networking tool to allow multiple users to enter the room. The final prototype allowed users to join a meeting room together, speak to one another, and see each other head and controller movements.

 
 

VR Buddy, VR Application: One of my first projects I started in a VR class in college, VR Buddy is an game highly inspired by "Interactive Buddy" - a web game I played and loved as a kid. For the project I worked with ragdoll kinematics, particle systems, and complex grabbing mechanics. I modeled 20+ different weapons for the game as well.
